FAQ

Q: How do I lock up my bulls? 

A: First, connect your MetaMask wallet to The Grill dApp. Then navigate to “The Grill” and select the bulls you would like to lock up. Once you’ve checked the boxes for the bulls you’d like to lock up, click “Lock My Bulls” and approve the transaction in MetaMask

Q: How long will it take to earn a burger?

A: During the initial cook and searing period, one burger will be emitted every 45 days per bull locked

Q: How many bulls can I lock up? 

A: As many as you can wrangle! There’s no limit to how many bulls you can grill

Q: What happens if I unlock my bulls before the 45 period has passed? ​

A: If you unlock your bulls before their 45 day grilling period has passed, you will not receive burgers for that period. If your bulls have been locked longer than 45 days, you will only receive burgers for the completed grilling periods.

Q: Can I lock and unlock multiple bulls at the same time?

A: Yes! You may lock and unlock as many bulls as you like, whenever you like.

Q: What can I do with my burgers?

A: We’re adding new utility fast, but you can use your burgers for special access to upcoming drops, access to the AstroVerse, and much, much, more! Check out our Discord for more!
