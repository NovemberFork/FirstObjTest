Instructions

To receive your burgers, first, lock up your bulls.

The Grill will be active, at high temperature, for an initial cook and searing period of 97 days. This will begin on May 9th at 00:00 UTC, and end 00:00 UTC on August 14th.

The Grill will close to new lockups on June 30th. The closure will be announced in advance.

The current emissions schedule will issue 1 burger per bull locked for 45 days. You may lock up as many bulls as you like.

You may unlock your bulls at any time, but unlocking a bull before its 45 day grilling period has passed will result in receiving no burgers for that period.
